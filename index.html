Personal Statement

I grew up during a time when technology was rapidly evolving, which allowed me to explore computers and mobile devices from a young age. As a child, I loved playing games, but soon my curiosity grew beyond just playing them. I began asking myself how games were designed, how systems worked behind the scenes, and what made them enjoyable for players. This curiosity led me to start exploring the world of technology. In high school, I began developing my skills more seriously by learning Web Development and Game Development. I created my own websites and experimented with building games using Unity, where I used C# to control game mechanics and systems. Through these projects, I discovered that programming is not only about writing code but also about solving problems and creating experiences for users. I also enjoy participating in technology projects and competitions because they allow me to collaborate with people who share similar interests and learn from different perspectives. What started as a childhood curiosity has grown into a passion, and I am motivated to continue improving my skills and creating innovative solutions that can benefit others.
